One day, I was hanging out with my friend Antoine, and I started playing some music from my phone.

My Spotify library consists mostly of R&B, pop and (just the right amount of) Ariana Grande. Antoine’s music taste, on the other hand, is a bit more eclectic; he listens to a lot of indie, Jean Leloup and French rap. Knowing this, I had a bit of trouble queuing songs I knew we would both like.

I started to wonder – is there a way to take the guessing out of this? What if I could make a playlist that, with higher certainty, catered to both of our music tastes?

I decided to explore these questions with a bit of machine learning, web dev, the Spotify API and voilà – my first web app! This demo generates playlists of Spotify songs that Antoine would have a high probability of liking, using an ML model trained on his music taste. With the option of selecting a playlist mood and several genres, the app returns up to ten songs.

Try it out and let me know what you think! I'd love to hear your thoughts. 😊
